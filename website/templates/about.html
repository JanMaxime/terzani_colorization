About the website

Purpose

Through this project, we try to bring the photographs taken during Mr. Terzani's trip to South Asian countries to an online platform and present them to the 21st-century digital audience. The Cini Foundation has made available nearly 8000 photographs, and you can explore them in the below ways.

Landmarks and Country

On the gallery page, you can click on countries on the map available, and the images associated with the selected country are displayed. Only those images that have country information are made available. At the same time, google vision API has detected landmarks in the photographs, and they can be retrieved by clicking on the show landmarks button.

Search by text

On the search page, you can search through the photographs taken by Mr. Terzani and his team during his travel by searching for some keywords like a car, horse, temple, etc. On clicking the image, you can search for photographs similar to the selected one and also colorize it. The photograph's IIIF annotation is also displayed.

My Terzani

Are you curious to see if you have clicked photos similar photos as Mr. Terzani or his team? Or do you want to search for the type of photograph in the collection? You are heading in the right direction. On the search page, you can upload an image and get similar photos from the collection..
